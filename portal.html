The Day

Your Itinerary

Dress Code

Elegant / Professional.

Think refined and celebratory — suits, cocktail dresses, and polished attire. Soft greens, champagne, and ivory tones complement the day beautifully, though you're welcome in any color that makes you feel your best. Comfortable dress shoes are wise for a day with a few stops.

Parking

Complimentary parking is available at each location. Canton House offers a large lot on-site for the evening reception. For the morning tea ceremonies, please use street parking or driveways as directed by family. Carpooling between the homes is encouraged — it's a short, easy drive.
